Extraplaneteers

Three.js Blender JavaScript HTML CSS

Project Overview

Extraplaneteers is an educational web platform that excites children about space exploration by allowing them to interact with 3D models of tools, robots, and satellites from real missions. By blending engaging visuals with informative content, it makes space science accessible and inspiring for young learners.

Notably, Extraplaneteers secured second place in the NASA Space Apps Hackathon, highlighting its innovative approach to STEM education.

Live Demo View Code

Key Features

Interactive 3D Models

Fully interactive 3D models of space tools, rovers, and satellites that users can rotate, zoom, and explore from every angle in their browser.

Educational Content

Each model comes with age-appropriate descriptions and facts about the tool's purpose, the missions it was used for, and its importance in space exploration.

Mobile-Friendly Experience

Responsive design ensures that the 3D models and educational content are accessible on tablets and smartphones, perfect for classroom use or learning on the go.
